About Me

As a creative and experienced professional in UX, web, and graphics designs, I understand the power of engaging applications to produce incredible results! I obsess myself over UI without losing the focus of overall big picture. Creating visually compelling and beautiful websites is only the tip of the iceberg! A key focus of mine is making these technologies humane, meaningful, and functional for the user. My area of expertise lies in frontend development, UX design, and graphic design. As a web performance architect, I work in many technical ecosystems for Enterprise Cloud Monitoring platforms. I recently received my certification demonstrating my knowledge in monitoring end to end environments starting with data centers, host, processes, services, to the applications layer. My current role along with my previous career path has given me a keen of understanding of web infrastructures and the user’s experience. In my spare time you can find me crafting art compositions in the non-technical arena!
